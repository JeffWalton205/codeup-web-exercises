Is there buried treasure?
Yes! Legend has it, the Treasure of Ol' Whistler was buried around Phantom Pass. Treasure hunters beware, though, because the Whistler's ghost protects his gold to this very day!
Where does Bigfoot live?
Several families of Bigfeet have a community around Crystal Cove and the adjacent Copper Cave region of the park. Challenge them to feats of strength if you dare but be forewarned: Bigfeet are known to rip peoples arms and/or legs out of their sockets when they lose!
Are there any abandoned mine shafts or wells to look out for?
All over the place, be sure to keep a constant headcount of your little ones!
